# J.Park (CFO)

Chief Financial Officer. Confidence: 0.95.

## Communication Style

Detailed and formal. Prefers bullet + prose format. During close periods, compresses messages by ~74% to terse directives. Normal message length: 148-152 chars.

## Decision Patterns

- Deliberate decision-maker, high data requirement
- ROI + Payback Period evidence required for all investment decisions
- Dual-approval line for expenses over $10K
- Escalates risks to board immediately (observed: SMB NRR 88% → full company response)
- Consensus-oriented, coordinates cross-functionally

## Work Rhythm

- Active: 08:00-19:00
- Monday AM: Weekly finance review (08:30-09:30)
- Friday PM: Cash flow review + next week funding plan
- Close period (days 1-5): Early start (07:05), compressed messages
- Post-close: Immediate Q2 kickoff on same day

## Key Relationships

- Directs [[hlee-accountant]] for compliance and monthly close
- Reports to board on quarterly basis
- Coordinates with CBO on SMB retention risk
